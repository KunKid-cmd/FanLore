Help & FAQs

🔹 What is FanLore?

FanLore is a creative platform where users share fandom-based content such as stories, fan art, game updates, and event creations. It's a collaborative space for creators and fans to engage and connect.

🔹 How do I become a content creator?

All registered users can post content and leave comments. However, only approved creators can host events and assign achievements.

To apply for creator access, contact our admin team at admin@fanlore.com with your request and a brief reason. The team will review your application and upgrade your permissions if approved.

🔹 How do I post content?

After logging in, click "Create Post" from your dashboard. You'll be able to add a title, description, tags, optional image, collaborators, and attach files if needed.

🔹 Can I edit or delete my content?

Yes! Navigate to your content detail page. If you're the creator or a collaborator, you'll see Edit and Delete buttons in the top-right corner.

🔹 What are releases?

Releases are versioned updates or downloadable files related to a content post. Each release may include a description, patch notes, changelogs, and multiple file attachments.

🔹 How do I manage releases?

Go to your content's detail page and click "Add Release". You can view, edit, or delete existing releases. Release sections are collapsible and support files via Cloudinary.

🔹 What are achievements?

Achievements are rewards event creators can assign to participants. They are shown on the user's profile and can only be given manually by the event host.

🔹 How do I create an event?

Creators can go to the dashboard and click "Create Event". You'll fill out event details and set up to five custom achievements to award later.

🔹 How do I assign achievements?

Open the Event Dashboard after your event is live. There, you'll see all user submissions and can manually assign achievements to each participant.

🔹 How do I add or accept friends?

click the Friends link in the sidebar. You can manage all requests from that page.

🔹 Who can collaborate on content?

Only users on your friends list can be added as collaborators.

Content creators can assign collaborators while creating or editing content. Collaborators can help manage descriptions, files, releases, and more.

🔹 How does voting/liking work?

Each user can like a piece of content once. Click the 👍 Like button to vote. It will turn green when active and show the total count.

🔹 How do I bookmark content?

Click the icon on any content page to bookmark it. Your saved bookmarks can be accessed from your profile.

🔹 Can I report inappropriate content?

Yes. Every content detail page includes a 🚩 Report button. Provide a topic and reason, and our team will review it promptly.

🔹 How do I contact the FanLore admin team?

For questions, permissions, or issues, email us at admin@fanlore.com. We’re happy to help!

🔹 Need more help?

For general support, reach out via support@fanlore.com. We're here to assist you!
